## File naming
- Use format: `firstname-lastname.jpg` or `company-name.jpg`
- Recommended size: 200×200px minimum, square crop
- Format: JPG or PNG

## Photo tips for customers
- Face shot or half-body
- Wearing work gear, trucking hat, or company shirt
- In front of their truck or on a job site
- Good lighting, no sunglasses
